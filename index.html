About Naltar Valley

Naltar Valley is located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of northern Pakistan. Surrounded by majestic mountains, alpine forests, and colorful lakes, it is one of the most scenic destinations in the country.

The Beauty of Naltar

Naltar Valley is famous for its crystal-clear lakes such as Satrangi Lake, snow-covered peaks, lush green meadows, and vibrant autumn trees. The valley offers breathtaking landscapes in every season.

Visitors can enjoy hiking, photography, skiing in winter, and peaceful relaxation in the heart of nature.
