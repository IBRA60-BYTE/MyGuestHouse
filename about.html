Discover the Magic

A Valley of Seven Colors

Naltar Valley isn't just a destination; it's a hidden sanctuary in the heart of Gilgit-Baltistan. Known for its high-altitude lakes and the world-renowned "Satrangi Lake," the valley offers a palette of colors that change with every shift of the sun.

At Naltar Peak Guest House, we built our retreat to honor this landscape. We believe in sustainable tourism that allows you to experience the wild beauty of the mountains without sacrificing the comforts of home.

The Satrangi Experience

Located just a short jeep ride from our doorstep, the Rainbow Lake (Satrangi) is the crown jewel of the valley. Whether you visit in the lush green of summer or the golden hues of autumn, the crystal-clear waters reflect the soul of the Himalayas.
