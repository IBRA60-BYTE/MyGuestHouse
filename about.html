About Naltar Valley

Discover Naltar Valley

Naltar Valley is one of the most beautiful valleys in northern Pakistan, located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region. Surrounded by towering mountains, lush pine forests, and colorful alpine lakes, it is a paradise for nature lovers.

The valley is especially famous for Satrangi (Rainbow) Lake, which changes colors depending on the sunlight. During winter, Naltar becomes a popular skiing destination, attracting visitors from across the country.

Nearby Resorts & Hospitality

Visitors traveling to the northern areas often stay at famous resorts such as Shangrila Resort in Skardu, known for its stunning lake views and peaceful environment. The region offers a mix of luxury resorts, mountain lodges, and eco-friendly accommodations.

Whether you are looking for adventure, relaxation, photography opportunities, or a family vacation, Naltar Valley provides an unforgettable experience.
